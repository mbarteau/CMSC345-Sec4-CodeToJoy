Login Screen

  • To Login:
    1. Enter username and password into corresponding fields.
    2. Press Login
  • If you don't have an account press Create a new account

Create an Account

  1. Choose a non-existing username and enter it into the username field. (If it already exists, you will be prompted to choose another name)
  2. Choose a password
  3. Retype your password the same way it was entered
  4. Press Create Account and you will be logged in automatically to start the trip selection process.

Select locations

  1. Select all of the locations you wish to visit.
  2. Press the next button

Start location

  1. Select the location, from the list of locations that you already selected to be the starting location for your trip.
  2. Press the next button

End Location

  1. Select the location, from the list of locations that you selected to be in your trip to be the ending location for you trip. The starting location is omitted.
  2. Press the next button

Email screen

  • If you wish to receive an email with your trip, follow these optional directions:
    1. Enter the email address you wish to receive the email into the email field. (ex. example@domain.com)
    2. Enter the date in any format that you wish to begin your trip. (ex. "12/12/12", "Feb 13", "Christmas 2015")

Map the trip in Google Earth

  1. First follow the Email Screen directions if you wish to receive an email reminder. If you do not, leave the text fields blank.
  2. Click "Launch Path" to view your path in Google Earth automatically. Your trip will then be saved, and you will be logged out.
